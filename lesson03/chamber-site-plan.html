Site Name

Stars Hollow Chamber of Commerce. The name represents a chamber for the fictional city in the show Gilmore Girls, which was released in 2000.

Site Purpose

The Stars Hollow Chamber of Commerce is a guide to locals and city visitors to find what local businesses have to offer as well as advertisement for current events that the city is hosting. Tourists and locals can find information on Dosey's market, Luke's cofee shop, the Dragonfly Hotel, and so much more. They will also see fun attractions the townsfolk put together.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the contact information of local business owners in the town?
  • What events will be held this month and will they be held at the town square?
  • How can I make donations to the city?
